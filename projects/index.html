about

Digital Shamans is a startup company registered in England. In its core is quality and self-development oriented, client focused, flexible, diverse and efficient savvy team, which consists of game dev veterans and new-gen talent. Our work shows experience and skill sets acquired from working on numerous games in the last 10+ years. Our work ethic shows experience brought from small company studios, winners of "The Best Place to Work in the UK" and nominees to TIGA Award in equivalent category.
What do we do?
We raise artistic, emotional and production value of video games by helping in their development. We also concept design and create production quality, performance friendly game assets and vfx.

Jobs

Senior Environment Artist (UE4):

As a Senior Environment Artist Digital Shaman, you will be working remotely on fix term contract in very flexible and dynamically developing startup team.

Responsibilities:

Responsibilities often change in a fast-paced environment like ours, so this role includes, but is not limited to, the following: As a Senior Artist mentoring and guiding less experienced workmates. Being highly self managed person willing to take on challenges requiring personal development. Helping create 3D environments and level art for video games as a supporting external team. Creating 3D Assets. Taking part in developing working practices, pipelines and the culture. Closely collaborating with other 3d artists, contracted artists, art director, project manager and concept design team. Ownership - High level of creativity and independence; requires little oversight

Skills & Requirements:

Experience working with creative team previously. Experience working as Finance Manager in game development or other entertainment sector. Experience working with international clients. Experience in pitching projects and making financial proposals. Growth mindset.

Additional Information:

Opportunity to take a part in shaping studio's working practices and create your ideal way of working. Super friendly small working environment of passionate creative people who have growth mindset and approach others through understanding. Flexible working hours.

Operations Manager COO:

As a Chief Operating Digital Shaman, you will be working remotely as a right hand of CEO and FCO on day to day administrative and operational functions helping to execute our plan and vision and to turn dynamically developing startup team into 20 men strong studio.

Responsibilities:

Responsibilities often change in a fast-paced environment like ours, so this role includes, but is not limited to, the following: Representing our tribe's values and culture. Working closely with the Company Director and Finance Manager. Discussing business plans and potential opportunities. Operational Risk Management. Turning ideas into reality. Constant introducing critical thinking to the team. Bringing discipline and organization to our creative team. Internal (with team members) and external (with clients) communication. Taking part in developing working practices, pipelines and the culture. Proactively working on improving our operational system by leveraging experience from the industry at first. Create documentation of processes for others to follow as needed.

Skills & Requirements:

Critical thinking. High conscientiousness. Disagreeableness. Growth mindset. Experience working in video games industry and understanding creative environment. Experience working as COO. Experience working in both environments, office studio and home freelance.

Additional Information:

Opportunity to take a part in shaping working practices and your own way working environment. Flexibility in choosing exact responsibilities at early stage of team formation. Already super friendly working environment. Flexible working hours. We encourage and support continuous professional and personal development, around which our working culture is revolving.
